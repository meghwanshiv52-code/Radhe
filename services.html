Household & Home Shifting

Moving your home requires meticulous planning and care. We treat your belongings like our own, ensuring safe and timely delivery to your new address, whether local or interstate.

Key Features:

  • **Professional Packing:** Multi-layer, high-quality packing materials (bubble wrap, corrugated sheets, waterproof containers).
  • **Loading & Unloading:** Use of modern equipment (dollies, ramps) to handle heavy and fragile items safely.
  • **Transportation:** Dedicated fleet of closed-body trucks with GPS tracking for real-time monitoring.
  • **Disassembly & Reassembly:** Expert carpenters for handling furniture and appliances.

"Experience zero stress with our fully insured home shifting service. Your peace of mind is our priority."

Corporate & Office Moving

Minimize business downtime with our specialized corporate relocation service. We handle IT equipment, sensitive documents, and office furniture with speed and precision.

Key Features:

  • **IT Equipment Handling:** Anti-static packing for servers, computers, and networking gear.
  • **Document Management:** Labelled and indexed file boxes for quick retrieval at the new location.
  • **Flexible Scheduling:** Weekend or off-hour moving to ensure zero impact on office working hours.

Car & Bike Transport

We offer secure, door-to-door vehicle transportation services using specialized closed and open carriers.

Car Transport:

Use of specialized wheel chocks and safety chains to secure your car inside the carrier. Full insurance coverage against transit damage.

Bike Transport:

Customized wooden crating or steel framework to prevent scratches and dents during transit. We ensure the bike is handled by experienced personnel only.

Note: All vehicles are transported after a thorough pre-inspection checklist is completed.
