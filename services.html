Moving your home requires personalized attention. We ensure every item, from fragile ceramics to heavy appliances, reaches your new residence safely.

Our 6-Step Household Moving Process:

  1. Pre-Move Survey: Free in-home survey to assess volume, special needs (AC removal, TV mounting), and prepare a custom quote.
  2. High-Grade Packing: Use of multi-layer protection (Air-bubble wrap, edge guards, stretch film) for maximum shock resistance.
  3. Inventory Check: Detailed digital list created for every packed box, ensuring nothing is missed.
  4. Loading & Transit: Use of specialized lifting tools. Items are secured inside closed-body trucks with trained drivers.
  5. Unloading & Placement: Items are unloaded and placed in the designated rooms as per your instruction at the destination.
  6. Unpacking & Reassembly: Complete unpacking of boxes and reassembly of all furniture (beds, wardrobes) and basic appliances.

Global moves made simple! We handle the complexities of borders, customs, and global logistics for a smooth transition.

Phase 1: Pre-Shipping Documentation

Navigating customs can be tough. We assign a dedicated coordinator to manage:

  • Customs Clearance: Handling permits, duties, and all necessary paperwork for India and the destination country.
  • Valuation & Insurance: Full Marine/Air Transit insurance options tailored for long-distance, high-value goods.
  • Inventory Listing: Detailed, multi-lingual inventory list required by international agencies.

Phase 2: Packing & Freight Options (Courier vs. Cargo)

Air Freight (Courier):

Faster delivery, ideal for smaller volumes, urgent documents, and high-value items. Uses specialized, lightweight, but durable cartons.

Sea Freight (Cargo):

Cost-effective for large household volumes. Options include FCL (Full Container Load) or LCL (Less than Container Load).

Phase 3: Final Delivery & Unpacking

We work with trusted international partners to ensure door-to-door service, including destination customs clearance and final placement in your new global home.
