International Courier & Cargo Moving

Your international move is managed by our global logistics experts, ensuring compliance and speed.

The International Process:

  1. **Pre-Move Assessment:** Discussing destination customs regulations and freight options (Air vs. Sea).
  2. **Export Documentation:** Managing the necessary shipping bills, packing lists, and Certificate of Origin.
  3. **Consolidation & Freight:** Arranging FCL (Full Container Load) or LCL (Less than Container Load) for Sea Cargo, or expedited Air Freight for urgent items/courier.
  4. **Customs Clearance:** Handling both Indian Export Customs and Destination Import Customs clearance.
  5. **Destination Delivery:** Door-to-door service, including final placement in your new country.

We eliminate the stress of global bureaucracy, offering comprehensive Marine or Air Insurance specific to international transit risks.
